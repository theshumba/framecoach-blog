You can make a professional film without spending a dollar on software. Here's the free tool for every need.

## Screenwriting
**WriterSolo** — Professional screenplay formatting in your browser. No account needed.

## Storyboarding
**Storyboarder** — Desktop app from the makers of Shot Reverser. Draw quick storyboards with automatic shot numbering.

## Camera
**Blackmagic Camera** (iOS) — Full manual controls and ProRes recording. Free and professional-grade.
**Open Camera** (Android) — Manual controls for Android devices.

## Camera Coaching
**[FrameCoach](https://framecoach.io)** — Real-time coaching through camera settings and composition. Built for indie filmmakers.

## Editing
**DaVinci Resolve** — The gold standard. Used on Hollywood films. Free version handles 95% of what you need: editing, color grading, audio mixing (Fairlight), and basic VFX (Fusion). This single app replaces Premiere, After Effects, and Audition.

## Audio Editing
**Audacity** — Record and edit audio. Remove noise, normalize levels, export clean dialogue.

## Sound Effects
**Freesound.org** — Massive library of Creative Commons sound effects.
**BBC Sound Effects** (for personal/educational use) — Thousands of high-quality SFX.

## Music
**YouTube Audio Library** — Royalty-free music filtered by mood, genre, and duration.
**Pixabay Music** — Free music with Creative Commons license.
**Incompetech** — Kevin MacLeod's extensive library (credit required).

## Color Grading
**DaVinci Resolve** — Free. The same color tools used on major films. Includes node-based grading, curves, wheels, and LUT support.

## Transcoding
**Handbrake** — Convert between video formats. Compress for web delivery.

## Distribution
**YouTube** — Largest audience, free hosting, searchable.
**Vimeo** (basic free plan) — Filmmaker-focused community.
**FilmFreeway** — Festival submission platform (free profile, festivals set their own fees).

## Project Management
**Notion** (free) — Production calendars, crew contacts, location notes.
**Google Drive** (15GB free) — Script sharing, call sheets, production documents.

The tools have never been better or more accessible. The only investment required is your time and creativity.
