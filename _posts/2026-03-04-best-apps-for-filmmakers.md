These 12 apps cover every aspect of film production from your phone or tablet.

## Camera & Coaching
1. **[FrameCoach](https://framecoach.io)** — Real-time camera coaching for settings, composition, and visual storytelling. Like having a DP in your pocket.
2. **Blackmagic Camera** (free, iOS) — Full manual camera controls, RAW recording on supported iPhones.
3. **Filmic Pro** ($15) — Advanced manual video controls for iPhone and Android.

## Pre-Production
4. **WriterSolo** (free) — Professional screenwriting format on mobile.
5. **ShotLister** ($25) — Shot list creation and management with scheduling.
6. **Storyboarder** (free, desktop) — Quick storyboard sketching.

## On-Set
7. **Artemis Director's Viewfinder** ($30) — Preview framing with different lenses and cameras virtually.
8. **Sun Seeker** ($10) — AR sun tracking for lighting planning.
9. **MovieSlate** ($30) — Professional digital clapperboard.

## Post-Production
10. **LumaFusion** ($30, iOS) — Professional editing on iPad.
11. **DaVinci Resolve** (free, desktop) — The industry standard for editing and color.

## Reference
12. **IMDB** (free) — Research crew, cast, and techniques from films you admire.

## How to Choose

Focus on your weakest area. If camera settings confuse you, start with FrameCoach. If editing is your bottleneck, master DaVinci Resolve. Don't install 20 apps — pick 3-4 that address your actual needs and learn them deeply.
