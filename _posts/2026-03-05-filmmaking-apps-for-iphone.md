Your iPhone is a cinema camera that fits in your pocket. These apps unlock its full potential.

## Camera Apps
- **Blackmagic Camera** (free) — Full manual controls, ProRes recording, false color, waveform. The best free option.
- **Filmic Pro** ($15) — Manual controls, dual recording, live analytics.
- **Apple Cinematic Mode** (built-in) — Automated rack focus with simulated depth of field.

## Camera Coaching
- **[FrameCoach](https://framecoach.io)** — Real-time coaching through camera settings and composition decisions. Designed for filmmakers learning on any device.

## Editing
- **LumaFusion** ($30) — The most powerful mobile editor. Multi-track, keyframing, color correction.
- **CapCut** (free) — Quick edits, good effects, easy to learn.
- **iMovie** (free) — Apple's basic editor. Limited but functional for simple projects.

## Audio
- **Voice Record Pro** (free) — Record high-quality audio separately for syncing.
- **Ferrite Recording Studio** (free basic) — Multi-track audio editing on iOS.

## Production
- **ShotLister** ($25) — Professional shot list management.
- **Sun Seeker** ($10) — Plan natural lighting with AR sun tracking.
- **Cadrage** ($10) — Director's viewfinder with lens simulation.

## Color & LUTs
- **VideoLUT** ($4) — Apply LUTs directly to footage on your phone.

## The Minimal iPhone Filmmaking Setup
You only need three apps to start: **Blackmagic Camera** (shoot) + **FrameCoach** (learn) + **LumaFusion or DaVinci Resolve on desktop** (edit). Everything else is optional.
