Here's every tool you need for indie filmmaking in 2026, organized by production stage. Most are free or affordable.

## Pre-Production
- **WriterSolo / Highland** (free) — Screenwriting with proper formatting
- **Canva** (free) — Storyboards, call sheets, posters
- **Google Sheets** (free) — Scheduling, budgets, contact lists
- **ShotLister** ($25) — Shot list management on iPad/iPhone

## Production
- **[FrameCoach](https://framecoach.io)** — Real-time camera coaching on set
- **Blackmagic Camera** (free, iPhone) — Pro manual camera controls
- **Sun Seeker** ($10) — Track sun position for lighting planning
- **Clapperboard apps** (free) — Digital slate for syncing audio

## Post-Production
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free) — Editing, color grading, audio mixing, VFX
- **Audacity** (free) — Audio editing and noise reduction
- **Handbrake** (free) — Video transcoding and compression

## Distribution
- **FilmFreeway** (free to create profile) — Festival submissions
- **YouTube/Vimeo** (free) — Online distribution
- **Canva** (free) — Marketing materials, social graphics

## Sound
- **Freesound.org** (free) — Sound effects library
- **YouTube Audio Library** (free) — Royalty-free music
- **Artlist** ($10/month) — Premium music and SFX

The best toolkit is the one you actually use. Start with free tools, upgrade only when you hit real limitations.
