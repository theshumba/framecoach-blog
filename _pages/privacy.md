# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

FrameCoach ("we", "us", "our") operates the website [framecoach.io](https://framecoach.io) and the FrameCoach Blog at [theshumba.github.io/framecoach-blog](https://theshumba.github.io/framecoach-blog/). This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Website Visitors
- **Analytics data:** We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes page views, referral sources, and general geographic region. No personally identifiable information is collected.
- **Cookies:** We use minimal cookies required for site functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

### App Users
- **Camera settings and preferences:** Stored locally on your device. We do not upload or access your camera settings data.
- **No account required:** You can use FrameCoach without creating an account or providing personal information.

## How We Use Your Information

- To improve the app and website experience
- To understand which content is most helpful to filmmakers
- To respond to questions or feedback you send us directly

## What We Don't Do

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use third-party advertising trackers
- We do not collect or store your camera footage or photos
- We do not require registration or personal data to use the app

## Third-Party Services

Our blog is hosted on GitHub Pages, which has its own [privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement). Our main site is hosted on Vercel, which has its own [privacy policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## Your Rights

You may contact us to request deletion of any personal data we may hold. Since we collect minimal data, in most cases we have nothing to delete.

## Changes
